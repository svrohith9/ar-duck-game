# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expectations
- Be respectful and considerate in language and actions.
- Focus on constructive feedback and collaboration.
- Assume good intent and be open to different perspectives.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, open an issue or contact the maintainer.

Thank you for helping keep this project welcoming and professional.
